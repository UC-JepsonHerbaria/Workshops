Code of Conduct & FAQ

All Participants are required to read the Code of Conduct & FAQ before registering for any workshops.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made at least one month or more prior to the workshop are eligible to receive a refund, less your $75 non-refundable deposit.
Cancellations made less than 30 days prior the workshop start date are not eligible for a refund.

Waitlist Process

If the workshop you would like to take is full, you can request to be placed on the waitlist. Simply send us your registration form and check “waitlist.” No deposit or course fee is required at the time of waitlisting.
We will contact you if a spot becomes available in the course you are waitlisted for.

Course Enrollment

All workshops require either the full course fee or a $75 dollar non-refundable deposit at the time of registration.

The remaining balance for your workshop fee is due no later than 30 prior to the workshop start, and we will send you a reminder approximately 6 weeks before your workshop that your balance is due. Failure to pay your balance by the balance due date risks you losing your place in the workshop to someone on the waitlist.

Please fill out one registration form per participant.

Note that by registering in the Jepson Herbarium Workshop program, you agree to adhere to our registration, cancellation and waitlist policies.
